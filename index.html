About

Dedicated researcher in computational materials

I'm a PhD Researcher at the Computational Materials Modeling (COMMIT) Lab at the University of Antwerp, Belgium. My work focuses on computational modeling of materials using density functional theory (DFT).

Previously, I worked as a Junior Research Fellow at the Materials Modeling and Simulation Laboratory (MMSL), where I developed expertise in VASP, GGA-PBE, and DFT+U methods for studying strongly correlated systems.
