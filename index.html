About Me

I am a researcher in computational materials science with a background in physics. My work focuses on understanding how the atomic structure of a material influences its electronic, optical, and transport properties.

I have worked as a Junior Research Fellow at the Materials Modeling and Simulation Laboratory, IIITDM Kancheepuram, on the project “Cost-effective and High-performance Transparent Conductors”. This work involved first-principles calculations to study and optimize functional materials for optoelectronic applications.

My research experience includes studying the effects of strain, defects, and transition-metal doping on material properties using density functional theory.
