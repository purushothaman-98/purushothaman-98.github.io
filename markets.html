Simple index framework

How I organise it

What to ownUse a low-cost NIFTY 50 index fund as the core and add only a NIFTY Next 50 index fund for broader large-cap exposure.

Simple allocationAn illustrative 75% NIFTY 50 and 25% NIFTY Next 50 split keeps the portfolio understandable while accepting some added volatility.

How to investContinue a fixed monthly SIP; use extra correction-based buying only from cash reserved in advance, never borrowed money.

Fund selectionPrefer a direct-plan fund with low tracking difference, stable tracking error, reasonable cost, and adequate scale—not the best recent return.

Realistic expectationUse roughly 8–10% annual nominal return only as a long-horizon planning assumption, not a promise.

Risk expectationExpect negative years, possible 30–50% equity drawdowns, and periods when NIFTY Next 50 substantially underperforms NIFTY 50.

Review ruleReview once a year, rebalance to the chosen split, and avoid switching funds because of short-term rankings or forecasts.
